About

I'm Russ Blackburn, a Principal UX Designer with over two decades of experience spanning advertising, B2C marketing, and enterprise software design. My career has taken me from crafting e-commerce experiences and print campaigns to designing complex SaaS applications used by enterprises worldwide.
