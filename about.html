About

I'm a Principal UX Designer at Red Hat with more than twenty years of experience designing digital products, customer experiences, and enterprise software.

My career began in advertising and visual design, where I developed a strong foundation in storytelling, branding, and customer engagement. Over time, my focus shifted toward user experience design, helping organizations solve complex business problems through research, interaction design, and strategic thinking.

Today I work on enterprise-scale experiences that support subscription management, customer portals, identity systems, onboarding journeys, and service experiences used by organizations around the world.
